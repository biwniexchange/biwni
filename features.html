Biwni Card

Unlock the potential and all the benefits of the exchange by using the Biwni Card.

The card is directly linked with a specific wallet from your account, which you can settle any time, and will let you buy goods and services worldwide just as any other debit card.

Shareholders with a minimum share of 0.1% of the exchange will receive a Golden Card for free which will include increased benefits.

The release of the Biwni Card is planned for early October.
